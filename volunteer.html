About Us

Meet Our Founder, Helen Young

Helen, the ultimate big sis to ten siblings, learned the art of caregiving early on. Though a bit of a picky eater in her teens, she discovered the power of healthy eating when she became a nurse. Working with kids, she saw firsthand the impact good nutrition could have. This sparked a passion that led her from the hospital scrubs to the world of early childhood education. There, she whipped up delicious, healthy meals for the little ones, and guess what? Less sniffles and happier parents all around.

Inspired by this success, Helen's journey blossomed into the Morning Family Garden! Here, she gets to share her love of healthy food and empower young people to grow their own organic produce and create nutritious meals they'll love.
